    Apply for Developer Access

    Thanks for your interest in becoming a developer of 0xTick community. In this tutorial, we will guide you to apply for beta access in 10 mins.

    Prerequisits¶

    In order to be invited as a developer, you must prepare information below:

    • your project website or working experience.
    • a valid discord account.

    Application¶

    Step 1: Join discord support group¶

    Join discord group chat: #townhall

    Step 2: Open a ticket for beta account¶

    type /open in textbox, and key in dev as the subject.

    Step 3: Providing information above¶

    Move to a newly created conversation to you and provide the information to apply.

    After reviewing, you will get username and password to access Event Studio.
